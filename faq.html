Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our legal advisory services

What types of legal issues does Justice Advisor handle?
Justice Advisor provides guidance on various legal matters including family law, property disputes, employment issues, consumer rights, criminal defense, and civil litigation. Our team specializes in Indian law and can advise on most common legal concerns faced by individuals and small businesses.
How is Justice Advisor different from hiring a traditional lawyer?
Justice Advisor combines legal expertise with technology to provide more affordable and accessible legal guidance. While we don't replace court representation, we offer preliminary advice, document reviews, and legal strategy consultations at a fraction of traditional legal costs. For cases requiring court appearances, we can refer you to qualified attorneys in our network.
What should I prepare for my initial consultation?
To make the most of your consultation, please prepare: (1) A clear summary of your legal issue, (2) Any relevant documents (contracts, notices, court papers), (3) A list of specific questions you want answered, and (4) Key dates related to your case. Organizing this information beforehandHelps us provide more targeted advice.
How quickly can I get legal advice through your service?
For urgent matters, we offer emergency consultations within 24 hours. Standard consultations are typically scheduled within 2-3 business days. Our document review service provides turnaround within 3-5 business days depending on complexity. We also offer a Quick Question service for brief inquiries with responses within 48 hours.
Is my personal information and case details kept confidential?
Absolutely. All communications with Justice Advisor are protected by attorney-client privilege to the fullest extent permitted by law. We use enterprise-grade encryption for all data and maintain strict confidentiality protocols. Your information will never be shared without your consent except as required by law.
Do you provide services for business legal matters?
Yes, we advise small businesses and startups on matters including company formation, contracts review, employment policies, intellectual property protection, and compliance issues. For complex corporate transactions or litigation, we can refer you to specialized business attorneys in our network.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and select digital wallets. Payment is required at the time of booking your consultation. For ongoing representation matters, we offer flexible payment plans that can be discussed during your initial consultation.
Can youHelp me if my case is already in court?
While Justice Advisor primarily provides legal guidance rather than court representation, we can certainly review your case,Help you understand court procedures, prepare legal documents, and develop effective strategies. For actual court appearances, we maintain a network of qualified litigators we can refer you to based on your specific needs.
Do you offer free consultations?
We offer a free 15-minute introductory call to understand your legal needs and determine if our services are right for you. Full consultations are fee-based as they involve detailed legal analysis and specific advice tailored to your situation. We believe this ensures you receive meaningful, quality guidance rather than superficial answers.
How do I know if I really need legal advice?
Consider seeking legal advice if: (1) You've received official legal notices or court documents, (2) You're entering into an important contract or agreement, (3) Your rights are being violated, (4) You're facing significant financial or personal consequences, or (5) You're unsure about legal requirements in a situation. When in doubt, it's better to consult - our advisors canHelp determine if formal legal action is needed.
